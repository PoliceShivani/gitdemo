SriDevi Women's Engineering College

Sridevi Women’s Engineering College (SWEC) is situated near Gandipet in the Rangareddy District of Andhra Pradesh and affiliated to JNTUH Hyderabad. At present, it offers undergraduate and postgraduate level programs viz. B.Tech. and MBA.

Seats are allocated on the basis of performance in the relevant entrance examination (TSEAMCET/ TSICET). Reservation is in accordance with regulations of the Government of India, with relaxations given in minimum qualifications as well. Students can apply through online mode with the relevant examination board or with the institute itself, as applicable.

Sridevi Women’s Engineering College (SWEC) offers admission to the MBA program on the basis of performance in the TSICET examination. Candidates should have passed at the undergraduate level from the relevant stream to be eligible to apply. Selected candidates must provide all original documents for verification for final admission to be processed.

Sridevi Women's Engineering College is situated in Hyderabad in Telangana state of India. Established in 2001, SWEC is a Private college. The college is accredited by AICTE, NBA.
